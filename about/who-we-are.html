Who We Are

PM Consulting is a strategic technology partner for businesses that want to move faster without sacrificing quality. We combine deep technical expertise with a people-first approach—whether we're building your next product, running your cloud, implementing SAP, or placing your next hire.

Our team is driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to tailored solutions. We don't do one-size-fits-all; we listen, plan, and deliver in a way that fits your culture, timeline, and budget. From startups to enterprises, we help you turn technology into a competitive advantage.

When you work with us, you get a partner who cares about your long-term success. We invest in relationships, not just projects—so you can focus on what you do best while we handle the rest.

Based in Indiana, we serve clients across industries and geographies. Our blend of IT consulting, software development, cloud engineering, and SAP expertise allows us to support you end-to-end—from strategy and implementation to ongoing support and talent. Who we are is simple: your extended team, committed to your success.
